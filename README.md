# Tonalitopia

A musical minigame where sound and gameplay are one and the same!

## About

- Tonalitopia is in chaos! The Continuous Controllers and their Midimites have taken instruments from all over the city, and it’s up to Modalman to listen close and get them back to their rightful Note Playing Characters (NPCs). Can you stop the Continuous Controllers before it’s too late?

- As there is no art currently:
    Midimites are spheres
    instruments are boxes
    NPCs are cylinders
    Modalman is a pill shape 

## Features

- **Dynamic Music System**: Built with Wwise, featuring 24 independently spatialized instruments
- **Interactive Audio**: Music responds to gameplay - instruments fade in/out and pan based on player position
- **Bitcrusher Effect**: Distance-based audio processing creates an immersive soundscape
